Mexican Chicken and Beans

Ingredients

  • 1 Pound of chicken breast strips.
  • 1 Package taco seasoning.
  • 1 Can black beans, drained and rinsed.
  • 1 Can corn, undrained.
  • 1 Bell pepper.
  • 1/4 Cup water.
  • Flour tortillas, if desired.

Step by Step

  1. Spray 10-inch skillet with cooking spray. Add chicken to skillet, cook over medium-high heat 8 to 10 minutes.
  2. Stir occasionally, until no longer pink in center.
  3. Stir in seasoning mix, beans, corn and water.
  4. Cook over medium-high heat 8 to 10 minutes, stirring frequently, until sauce is slightly thickened.
  5. Serve with tortillas.

Tips

  1. You can replace the black beans with pinto beans.
  2. If you cant find chicken stips, boneless chicken cut to strips will work as well.

Nutritional contents(pet serving)

340 Total calories, 5 g Total calories, 37 g Protein, 48 g Total Carbohydrate
