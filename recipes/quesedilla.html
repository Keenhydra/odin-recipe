Conky Chicken Quesedillas.

Ingredients

  • 6 oz seasoned chicken breast strips.
  • 1/2 cup Cunky Salsa.
  • 8 Flour Tortilla.
  • 2 Cups shredded cheese.
  • 1/4 Cups sour cream.
  • Cooking spray or butter.

Step By Step

  1. Cut chicken in small strips, mix in a bowl with salsa.
  2. Spray one tortilla and place it sprayed side down on the cooking surface.
  3. Fill with 1/4 of the chicken mixture and 1/2 cup of cheddar.
  4. Top with another tortilla, cooking spray on top.
  5. Repeat this for the remainder of the tortillas.
  6. Cook in skillet for 4 to 6 minutes till golden brown. Carefully turning every 2 minutes. Repeat with remaining tortillas.
  7. Cut in wedges and serve with sour cream, if desired extra salsa.

Tips

  1. Use a large enough skillet to make turning them over easier.
  2. If desired serve with guacamole and hot sauce.
  3. If you can't find seasoned chicken regular cooked chicken seasoned yourself will do.

Nutritional contents(per serving).

580 Calories, 38 g Total Fat, 29 g Protein, 25 g Total Carbohydrate of which 3 g Sugars
