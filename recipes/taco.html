Thundering Taco

Ingredients

  • One pound of lean ground beef.
  • One cup of Salsa.
  • 10 Taco shells.
  • 1/2 Head of lettuce.
  • Medium sized tomato.
  • One cup(aprox 4 ounces) shredded Cheddar cheese.

Step by step

  1. Cook beef in a skillet on medium heat, stirring it occasionally, for 10 minutes until brown.
    Add salt and peper if desired then drain excess oil.
  2. Shred the lettuce into fine pieces. Chop the tomato into small chunks.
  3. Stir salsa into beef, heat till boiling and turn down. Stir constantly.
    Cook for 5 minutes then pour beef into large serving bowl.
  4. Heat taco shells as directed on package.
    Serve this alongside the beef, lettuce, tomato and cheddar.

Tips

  1. You can serve extra condiments like chopped avocado, bell peppers or cilantro.
  2. Put some sauce alongside the condiments like Sour cream, guacamole or extra salsa.
  3. Try switching the ground beef for ground chicken or soy meat replacement.
